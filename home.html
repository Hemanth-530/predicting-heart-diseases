Predicting Heart Diseases Using Machine Learning and Different Data Classification Techniques...

Heart disease (HD), including heart attacks, is a primary cause of death across the world. In the area of medical data analysis, one of the most difficult problems to solve is determining the probability of a patient having heart disease. Death rates can be lowered by the early detection of heart diseases and the constant monitoring of patients by physicians..



The heart is a muscular organ that represents the central pumping organ of the circulatory system. It is responsible for pumping blood throughout the body and is one of the components of the cardiovascular system..
